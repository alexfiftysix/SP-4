Brisbane rockers Street Pieces play heavy, sexy rock with powerful vocals, screaming guitar, and a solid yet tantalizing rhythm section.

Street Pieces have been perfecting their craft playing with local bands Love Hate Rebellion, Former Angels and more, and international acts including Wolfmother, Shihad and The Bellrays.

Their sound has been described as spanning Led Zeppelin to INXS to Royal Blood.

Street Pieces' new EP Everything You Ever Wanted, produced by Jacob Laube, Julia Kourtidis and the band, mixed by Govinda Doyle and mastered by King Willy, is being launched November 30 at The Foundry.
